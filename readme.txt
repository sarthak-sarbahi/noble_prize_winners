The Nobel Prize is perhaps the worlds most well known scientific award. Except for the honor, prestige and substantial prize money the recipient also gets a gold medal showing Alfred Nobel (1833 - 1896) who established the prize. Every year it's given to scientists and scholars in the categories chemistry, literature, physics, physiology or medicine, economics, and peace.

In this analysis, we tried to capture insights on the following:
- Nobel Prize winners on the basis of gender and nationality
- Dominance of the USA in the Nobel Prize charts
- Individuals who won it more than once!
- Age of people when they won the Nobel Prize and their differences between prize categories
- Oldest and youngest winners
